--- Title ---

Core-Siphon: Among the Dead Stars

--- Author ---

Kyle Hebenheimer

--- Description ---

A primarily text based rpg in the vast and harsh expanse of space. Takes some inspiration from:
 Endless Sky for mechanics, Eve Online for missions / goals / wealth and economy, and 
Runescape / Jagex's project Mechscape that never materialized, for the skill / combat system but 
CLI based with the simple GUI layer to be added in later.

--- Mission Brief ---

Gain experience as you navigate the rigged atmospheres and terrains 
and work towards building your base of operations. Don't forget, you'll
need to level up and maintain your gear over time if you wish to gain
massive wealth and power in the endless hard frontier of space. Learn to
care for your tools and weapons, and they'll take of you, soldier.
